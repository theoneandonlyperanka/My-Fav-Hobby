My Favorite Hobby
Drawing

Hi! I'm Priyanka. I'm going to tell you about my favorite hobby.

Through drawing I feel I can express the various emotions I feel, train of thoughts, and collective feelings. Sometimes when I feel like I need to get away from life and relax, I draw, and it helps me a lot. Shy people, or people with verbal disabilities also find drawing a better communication to speak or interact with others.
